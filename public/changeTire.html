Change A Flat Tire

Steps

  • Step 1: Find a safe location.
  • Step 2: Turn on your hazard lights.
  • Step 3: Apply parking brake.
  • Step 4: Gather tire tools (wrench and jack). Reference car owner manual for tool location.
  • Step 5: Remove hubcap or wheel cover using wrench tool.
  • Step 6: Loosen lug nuts using wrench tool.
  • Step 7: Place jack under the vehicle.
  • Step 8: Raise vehicle with jack.
  • Step 9: Unscrew lug nuts using wrench tool.
  • Step 10: Reomve flat tire.
  • Step 11: Mount spare tire on lug bolts.
  • Step 12: Tighten lug nuts by hand.
  • Step 13: Lower vehicle and tighten lug nuts again using wrench tool.
  • Step 14: Replace hubcap or wheel cover.
  • Step 15: Stow all tools.
  • Step 16: Check tire pressure in spare tire.
  • Step 17: Take flat tire to a techician.
